# Higgsfield prompts for category hero images

Generate each image as a **photorealistic editorial still**, not a video frame with motion blur.

Shared settings:

- Landscape 16:9, ideally 2400 × 1350 or larger.
- Premium Indian raw-material trading aesthetic.
- Natural, controlled studio lighting with believable materials and containers.
- No people, hands, text, labels, logos, watermarks, floating objects or impossible glassware.
- Avoid glossy “AI luxury”, fantasy laboratories, wellness-ad clichés and excessive depth of field.
- Keep the main subject weighted toward the right half with calmer negative space on the left for responsive cropping.
